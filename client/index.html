About
This mini-game was created as a capstone project for my foundation at DevMountain. The idea is to create an RPG battle card game. My goal was to show that I can build a solo project from the scratch, based on the knowledge I learn during my foundation course. The process was challenging but fun. I add some cool CSS animation and sound effects so users can experience the whole feel of the theme of my game.
Rules
1. Create your account and hit submit

2. Hit the "ENTER HERE" button to open the character selection window

3. Select your character by pressing the "SELECT" button

4. Next you need to have an enemy opponent, click "JOIN BATTLE" to reveal your opponent

5. Click at "ATTACK" button to start a duel

6. Click the "PLAY AGAIN" button to reset the game
